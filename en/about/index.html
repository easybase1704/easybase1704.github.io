Company Profile

Shenzhen EasyBase Technology Co., Ltd. is a high-tech enterprise specializing in the R&D, manufacturing and sales of industrial computers, cloud terminals and edge computing devices. With proprietary product lines covering both X86 and domestic platforms, our products are widely used in education, healthcare, finance, transportation and manufacturing industries, delivering complete hardware-to-software solutions.

Headquartered in Yunzhi Science Park, Guangming District, Shenzhen, we have a professional R&D team and complete production system. ISO 9001 certified, we hold numerous national invention patents, utility model patents and software copyrights. We are committed to becoming a leading provider of integrated intelligent business hardware and software solutions.
